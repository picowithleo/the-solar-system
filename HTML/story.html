Kid, we are currently on Mercury!

This is the planets closest to the Sun!

Mercury is known for its many giant craters that cover the planets.

This is Venus, the Second Planet from the Sun!

Venus is known for being similar to the Earth in size and mass!

Kid, if you want to see a planet in the night sky, Venus is the brightest of them all from Earth!

This planet is known as the Red Planet because it has a red glow from Earth!

This giant planet is the largest within the Solar System!

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun!

Look kid! Now we have landed ourselves on Saturn!

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun!

It is known for its many rings that surround the giant planet!

Look out our Window kid!

Uranus is unique because it spins on its side!

Finally we have Neptune, kid!

This is the furthest planet from the Sun.
