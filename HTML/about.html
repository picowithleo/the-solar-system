About Author

Jinyuan Chen is the author of this website. He is an international student who has been in Australia for nearly four years. Now he continues to pursue a Master of Information Technology at University of Queensland.

About Website

This website is designed for the specific target audience aged between 8 and 13 years old in the group for young children and teenagers, who has an interest in knowing the universe and reading stories. Audience who under 10 should use this website.

The design theme of the website is in an education content, which aims to teaching audiences something. Therefore, for young children and teenagers’ audience, auhtor thought an educational content themed with recognisable characters or stories could have a good effect on improving engagement in their learning experience.

Through this website to learn about foundation knowledge of the solar system such as its formation, planets, culture, discovery and so on, to create a reminder of the vast collection of planets in the solar system for the audiences.



About Story

The story in this website is “The Solar System” which is written by Jennifer Aspenson. It is about an alien taking a trip in the solar system to explore the planets one by one from the sun.

When passing through the Earth, it finds a group of lovely children and invites them to get in its spaceship and have a trip together. Children will receive information for each major planet of the solar system from the alien.
